Welcome to Syllabus Analyzer

As part of our B.Tech in Information Technology, we have developed Syllabus Analyzer, a smart platform that helps students and teachers efficiently track syllabus progress, access curated study materials, and receive timely notifications to stay ahead academically.

Intelligent Progress Tracking

Visualize your syllabus completion with interactive bar graphs and AI-powered predictions, making it easy to understand your academic progress.

Timely Alerts & Notifications

Receive instant pop-up alerts if your syllabus progress falls behind schedule, so you can take corrective action immediately.

Curated Learning Materials

Access handpicked reference links and study materials tailored specifically for each syllabus topic to enhance your learning experience.

About the Project

Syllabus Analyzer is a full-stack application designed to bridge the gap between students and educators by providing:

  • Real-time syllabus progress monitoring
  • Automated, personalized notifications for lagging progress
  • AI-driven insights predicting completion trends
  • Secure user authentication with role-based access for students and teachers
  • Easy access to learning resources and reference materials

Our goal is to create an efficient academic management tool that empowers users to take charge of their learning journey with smart technology.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Spring Boot (Java) / Node.js with Express
  • Frontend: Angular / React / Vue.js for responsive UI
  • Database: PostgreSQL / MySQL for data persistence
  • AI Integration: Machine learning models for syllabus progress prediction
  • Testing: Unit and integration testing to ensure reliability
